Project Overview

Problem Statement: Today, the lack of trees in urban environments contributes to several environmental problems that we must begin to act on, such as air pollution, the urban heat island effect, and reduced biodiversity. Identifying areas with a high need for trees or green infrastructure is crucial to effectively mitigate these issues and improve the quality of life for residents.

Vision: Empower individuals, communities, and organizations to collaborate effectively and combat climate change and deforestation.
